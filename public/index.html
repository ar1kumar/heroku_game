How to Play?

  • 1. Connect your phone as a controller
  • 2. Use the unique room ID to start your gaming session
  • 3. Once both players are ready, click on PLAY
  • 4. Hold your phone(near the hip) like a gun by pointing it towards the ground
  • 5. Wait for the countdown to reach 0
  • 6. To fire your gun draw your phone from the current position and point it towards your opponent
